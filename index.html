About me

Hi! Welcome to my website. My name is Paulo, and I'm an engineering student from Barcelona, Spain!

Right now, I am finishing my undergraduate in Geoinformation and Geomatics Engineering at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia. These studies have led me to work as a trainee in Geodesy at the Cartographic Institute of Catalonia (ICGC), as well as doing research on Planetary Science at Jacobs University Bremen (JUB) in Germany.

These two experiences have helped me improve my programming skills. At the ICGC, I adapted some websites and used Python to read GNSS receivers' signals, organising the information and creating charts on it. At JUB, I used Machine Learning to create ice masks on Mars's surface and prepared the data to be further analysed.

Since I started the degree my interest in programming and Earth and planetary science has been becoming bigger and bigger, leading me to strongly consider studying my Master's related to Machine Learning and Computer Vision, hoping to apply them to my geospatial knowledge.
